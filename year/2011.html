Well, it took us nine years, but we are back, y’all! As you can tell from our photos, time has not been kind to us both. Mark has been consumed with big and small game hunting, while I have been "keepin' it real" relaxing at my crib at the Drippy Pines Trailer Park (where the living is both easy and gracious). Thanks to all who sent Get Well cards for Mr. Poopy – most of you know that he lost his left opposable foot thumb in a street brawl protecting my honor. Merry Christmas!

We're Ba-aaack! Maybe it's just a coincidence, but the last Psychotic Christmas went out the December before our twins were born. Happily, I have a lot more free time now that the girls spend 12 hours a day at that sewing factory in the Garment District. By the way, after listening to the Parks and Recreations clip that Charlie found for this year's playlist, I also only refer to eggs as either "pre-birds" or "future birds".

I hope you enjoy the tracks we selected and have a Merry Christmas!
