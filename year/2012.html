Dear Constant Listener...it has been a very fruitful year. Mark finally finished his online surgical training in the neurological sciences and guess what? I was his first patient. Ever since I was a young lad, I have always wanted to experience trepanation (tattoos and piercings are for suckers!) Since the procedure, I have been enjoying the holiday season with glee and grandeur. I have been splitting my time between helping Mrs. Claus squeeze Santa's boots on in the morning and training Wes and Gabby to wrangle the neighborhood fauna (for the stewpot of course!) I hope everyone has a great holiday season. See you next year.

Merry Christmas, everyone! It's been an interesting year, what with our move to Orange County and my having (barely) completed an online correspondence course in Brain Surgicality. Aware of my less-than-stellar academic performance, Charlie was kind enough to help me work in a little practice before last quarter's mid-term and, in Charlie's own words, "My affected wasn't negatively brain at all."

I hope you like this year's playlist. We have a lot of new stuff from some relatively obscure bands, as well as a few altie classics. Of special note is the Outer Spaces track (Walking Backwards), which my friends Jonathan and Dave released earlier this year. Enjoy!
