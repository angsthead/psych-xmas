Comments

Here's a little added bonus: a copy of my "Have Yourself an Angstful Xmas" mix tape from 1993. This was before CD burners were available to the public, but I was able to master the compilation on CD-quality digital audio tape, which is why the tracks sound so good. Jonathan helped out a lot on this one. We jacked a VHS player into his studio to import the the Heat Miser and Snow Miser songs from a video tape and brought in Charlie's turntable to digitize tracks from vinyl that weren't available on CD at the time. "Antarctica" is a guilty pleasure of mine, by the way. All my music-snob friends give me crap for liking this song, but come on, when the guy sings "Antarcticaaaaa-oo!" it's just so awesome. It just don't get any more 80's than that, folks.
